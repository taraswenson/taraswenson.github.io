ABOUT

Hi! My name is Tara. I'm an instructional designer/motion designer based outside Olympia, WA. My background is in video production and motion graphics, but in 2017 I transitioned into instructional design. I enjoy creating robust and engaging instructional content, and making highly technical content accessible to learners of all backgrounds.

When I'm not developing educational content, I'm recording music, hanging out with my partner, son, and our two pups, or building my permaculture garden.

To learn more about my experience, or to view my references, reach out to me at taradswenson@gmail.com.

Thanks for visiting my site!
